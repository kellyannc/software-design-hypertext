Aladdin Sane, 1973

Aladdin Sane followed on the heels of Bowie's 1972 masterpiece, The Rise and Fall of Ziggy Stardust and the Spiders from Mars. Aladdin Sane is Bowie again at his finest. Bowie's career took off with 1971 Hunky Dory and was propelled forward with every year that went by in the 70s. It was also some of Bowie's darkest years.
